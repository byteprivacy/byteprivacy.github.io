About BytePrivacy

After one semester’s EAP course: Digital Identity in Modern Public Spheres, we learned and researched on several topics about human in digital era, including concepts like anonymity, doxing, artificial intelligence and privacy.

I picked Online Privacy as my focus, and searched for multiple comments, academic journals, surveys and solutions, in order to better understanding why privacy is important to us, and how to better protect our privacy in the era of large data collections.

Thank you for visit this demo website. Looking forward to providing you with any help on personal privacy in big data era!
